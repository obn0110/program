Fraud Protection

The customer's IP address is logged in at the time of order placement. This is done for reasons of fraud protection.

In case of fraudulent orders, they are identified and submitted to the appropriate authorities.

A customer has the right to visit the Box8’s web page without submitting any personal identifiable information.

The web servers merely collect the domain names and not personal information of the customer.

Box8 reserves the right to collect personal information from the customer, including but not limited to first and last name, telephone & mobile numbers, email address, delivery address & any other information voluntarily provided by the customer.

This information is collected in the event a customer is:

• Placing an order on the website/app or through other platforms.
• Entering a contest or promotional activity.
• Participating in a survey.
• Subscribing to the brand’s mailing list.
• Applying for a job.
• Submitting website/app feedback.
• Personal information collected by Box8 from any other source will be duly informed.

Use and Disclosure

Apart from the events mentioned above, Box8 may use the personal information collected to improve the content of the website/app, customize the website/app to the customer's preferences, communicate information to the customer, and for any other specified purposes.

omino may also store and process the personal information collected in order to provide goods and services.

The personal information is used in accordance to the customers benefit, including but not limited to any of the following purposes:

• Provide the customer with outlet or promotion information.
• Process an online order placed by the customer.
• Determine the number of unique customers the website/app receives on any given day.
• Conduct reviews of the website/app.
• Fulfill prizes, awards and purchases.
• Respond to specific requests from customers.
• Keep the customers informed of any changes to the website/app.
• Conduct market research.
• Periodically send offers and information on products and services that Box8 deems will be of interest to the customers.

Further information on the terms of use of personal information used during online ordering is mentioned below:

Additional customer information is collected at the time of placing an online order on the omino website/app.
This is done to verify customer credentials and facilitate delivery to the customer's door.
The online ordering system stores information about the customers past orders in order to enable quick re-ordering in the future.
On concluding an online order on the brand’s website/app, the customer may be prompted to associate their contact details like mobile number with their social media accounts such as Facebook, Google, etc. The customer may choose to do so for ease of future ordering.
